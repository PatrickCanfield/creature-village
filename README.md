## Development Roadmap
### Basic Menus
Simple menu rendering, highly minimalistic and most likely filled with placeholders due to abscense of player and town.
### Town
Acre generation, terrain and object tile interpereting and rendering, town creation, and town naming will come here.
### Player
Will be weaved in with the [[#Town]] step, and will introduce player naming, creation, rendering, and NPCs.
### Menu Rework
Will be weaved in with the [[#Town]] and [[#Player]] steps, and will introduce nice looking menus that take in data from the rest of the world.
### Saving
Saving, storing and loading town and player data.
### Making the Game Good
Doing basically everything else.
